Here is how you prepare this dish

INGREDIENTS

  • Thinly sliced beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Garlic (minced or crushed)
  • Ginger (minced or grated)
  • Cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • Red onions (thinly sliced)
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • Fish sauce
  • Chili (optional, for a spicy kick)

STEPS

  1. Marinate thinly sliced beef in a mixture of soy sauce, lime juice, minced garlic, and grated ginger for 30 minutes.
  2. Grill or pan-sear the beef until cooked to your liking. Set aside to cool.
  3. In a large bowl, combine sliced cucumbers, halved cherry tomatoes, thinly sliced red onions, and freshly chopped cilantro.
  4. In a separate small bowl, whisk together lime juice, soy sauce, minced garlic, grated ginger, and a touch of brown sugar for the dressing.
  5. Slice the cooked beef into thin strips and add it to the vegetable mixture.
  6. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
  7. Serve the Thai Beef Salad as a delicious and refreshing meal.
