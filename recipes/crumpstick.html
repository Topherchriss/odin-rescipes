INGREDIENTS

Here is what you need to get going!

  • 2 steaks (your preferred cut, such as ribeye or sirloin)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs (preferably panko)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Take the steaks out of the refrigerator and let them sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes. This helps ensure even cooking.
  3. Heat a cast-iron skillet or oven-safe pan over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil to the pan and let it get hot.
  4. Season the steaks generously with salt and black pepper on both sides.
  5. Place the steaks in the hot skillet and sear them for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until you achieve a nice brown crust. This will depend on the thickness of your steaks and your desired level of doneness.
  6. While the steaks are searing, prepare the garlic crumb mixture. In a bowl, combine the minced garlic, breadcrumbs, grated Parmesan cheese, and chopped parsley. Mix well.
  7. Remove the seared steaks from the skillet and place them on a cutting board or a plate. Let them rest for a few minutes.
  8. In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Once melted, add the garlic crumb mixture to the skillet and stir it around for a minute or two until the breadcrumbs become golden and crispy. Be careful not to burn them
  9. Place the seared steaks back into the skillet on top of the garlic crumb mixture. Press the mixture onto the top of each steak to create a crust.
  10. Transfer the skillet with the steaks into the preheated oven and cook for about 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or adjust the cooking time to your preferred level of doneness.
  11. Once the steaks are done, remove them from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute throughout the meat.
  12. Slice the steaks against the grain and serve them with the crispy garlic crumb topping. You can garnish with additional chopped parsley if desired.
